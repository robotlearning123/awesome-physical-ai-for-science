# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive community. Contributors are expected to:

- Be respectful in discussions and reviews.
- Focus feedback on ideas and code, not people.
- Assume good intent and seek clarification before escalation.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing or sharing private information.
- Repeated disruptive or hostile behavior.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues/PRs, or block contributors for violations.

To report violations, contact: conduct@labclaw.dev
